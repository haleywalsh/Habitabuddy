"Habit-a-buddy"

Habitabuddy is a habit tracking app that also tracks your mood. It is based in psychology. By identifying a habit, figuring out why you want to change, setting a reasonable goal, measuring your progress, and finding support, one is more likely to succeed in creating or breaking a habit in as little as three weeks. The color scheme of Habitabuddy also roots in the psychology. The colors are comforting, like a friend, but also positive and motivating.

The goal was to make the interface easy and self-explanatory for anyone to figure out and use. Adding colors and icon customization allows your Habitabuddy to be your own.

FAQ

How do I log an activity?

Press the plus button in the bottom center and select new entry.

How do I add a new habit?

Press the plus button in the bottom center and select new habit.

How do I delete a habit?

From the home screen, press on the habit you wish to delete, then on the next page press “delete habit” in the top right.

What is the benefit of tracking my mood as well?

By tracking one’s mood as well, you can see how completing tasks can improve your mood, therefore staying motivated.

Is there a limit to how many habits I can track?

Nope! Add as many as you would like!
